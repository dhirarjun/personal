About Me

Arjun Dhir is a professional bridge player with almost 15 years of experience. He has represented the USA internationally in China, Turkey, and Italy.

Career Highlights:

  • 15 years as a professional bridge player
  • International representation for the USA: China, Turkey, Italy
  • Numerous national and regional tournament victories

My Philosophy

“Bridge should never feel stressful — especially when you’re learning. My goal is to create an atmosphere that values good experiences, while not sacrificing results.”

Through years of competitive play, I focus on teaching and partnering in a way that balances skill, strategy, and enjoyment for every player.
